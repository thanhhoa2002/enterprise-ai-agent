COMPANY WORKING POLICY

1. GENERAL WORKING HOURS
Employees are expected to work from Monday to Friday, from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
A standard lunch break of one hour is provided between 12:00 PM and 1:00 PM.
Any changes to working hours must be approved by the direct manager and the HR department.

2. REMOTE WORK POLICY
The company allows employees to work remotely up to two days per week.
Remote work eligibility depends on job role, performance history, and team requirements.
Employees must submit a remote work request at least one day in advance.
Manager approval is required before remote work can be performed.
Remote work may be revoked if productivity or communication is negatively impacted.

3. ANNUAL LEAVE POLICY
Each full-time employee is entitled to twelve days of paid annual leave per year.
Annual leave accrues monthly and can be carried over to the next year with manager approval.
Employees must submit leave requests at least five working days in advance.
Emergency leave may be granted under special circumstances and must be documented.

4. SICK LEAVE POLICY
Employees are entitled to sick leave in accordance with local labor laws.
A medical certificate is required for sick leave exceeding two consecutive days.
Sick leave abuse may result in disciplinary action.

5. PERFORMANCE AND CONDUCT
Employees are expected to maintain professional behavior at all times.
Performance reviews are conducted twice a year by the direct manager.
Repeated failure to meet performance expectations may result in corrective action plans.

6. INFORMATION SECURITY
All employees must comply with company information security policies.
Confidential company data must not be shared with unauthorized parties.
Use of personal devices for work purposes must comply with IT security guidelines.
Violations of information security policies may lead to disciplinary measures.

7. TERMINATION POLICY
Either party may terminate the employment contract by providing written notice.
The notice period is thirty days unless otherwise specified in the employment agreement.
All company assets must be returned upon termination.

8. POLICY UPDATES
The company reserves the right to update or modify policies at any time.
Employees will be notified of significant policy changes via official communication channels.
